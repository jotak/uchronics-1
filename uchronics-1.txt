1402 A.D., Anatolia. After defeating the Ottomans in Aleppo, Damascus, Baghdad, Ankara... Tamerlan granted lands to his allies. Like Diyar Bakr. But not to the Aq Qoyunlu, the so-called "White Sheeps". The need stronger allies to keep their conquests. Lands are granted to the old ennemies, the Safavids.

In 1406, the Safavids help Timurids to keep control of Tabriz against "Black Sheeps".

From 1410 to 1450, Safavids and Timurids strenghten their alliance, giving a new life to the ancient Persian Empire.
